Overview

Paws In Peace (PIP) is a utility token and decentralized platform designed to revolutionize pet aftercare services. Combining blockchain transparency, community governance, and compassion, PIP empowers users to access premium pet cremation, digital memorials, 3D-printed urns, and eco-friendly tributes — while earning rewards and participating in a cause-driven ecosystem.

"To ensure every beloved pet has a dignified farewell, personalized memorial, and a place to rest in peace — never mixed with others, never forgotten."

How to Participate in Presale

Acquiring PIP tokens is simple! Follow these steps to join our mission:

  1. Set up your Wallet: Download and set up MetaMask (or your preferred Web3 wallet). Ensure it's connected to the Polygon Network.
  2. Fund your Wallet: Send MATIC (Polygon's native cryptocurrency) to your MetaMask wallet. You can buy MATIC from exchanges like Binance, Coinbase, or Kraken.
  3. Connect to our Presale Contract: Using MetaMask, send MATIC directly to the PIP token contract address: 0x1e1182d9c5673de368203f581f50bc0addc1225. The contract automatically sends PIP tokens to your wallet based on the presale rate (1 MATIC = 1000 PIP).
  4. Add PIP to MetaMask: To see your PIP tokens, add the token to MetaMask using the contract address.
  5. Hold and Contribute: Your PIP tokens will now be in your wallet. By holding and supporting PIP, you're directly contributing to animal welfare!
