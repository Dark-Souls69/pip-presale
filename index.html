A Revolution in Pet Aftercare

Paws In Peace (PIP) is a utility token and decentralized platform designed to solve the trust and transparency crisis in pet aftercare services. Combining blockchain's immutable ledger, community governance, and deep compassion, PIP empowers users to access premium, verified pet cremation, create lasting digital memorials, and participate in a cause-driven ecosystem.
